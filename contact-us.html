APJ Service Provider CTO Team

Driving Digital Transformation Across Asia Pacific & Japan

The Asia Pacific and Japan (APJ) Service Provider Chief Technology Officer (CTO) team at Cisco is dedicated to helping service providers navigate their digital transformation journey. We work closely with telecommunications companies, internet service providers, and cable operators across the region to architect and implement cutting-edge solutions that drive innovation and business growth.

Our Focus Areas

  • 5G Network Architecture and Deployment Strategies
  • AI-Powered Network Operations and Automation
  • Cloud-Native Infrastructure Transformation
  • Edge Computing and IoT Solutions
  • Network Security and Zero Trust Architecture
  • Sustainable Technology and Green Networks
  • Open RAN and Disaggregated Networks
  • Service Orchestration and Automation

Our team consists of seasoned technology leaders and architects with deep expertise in service provider networks, emerging technologies, and business transformation. We collaborate with service providers to design and implement solutions that enhance operational efficiency, improve customer experience, and create new revenue opportunities.
